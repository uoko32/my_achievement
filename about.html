Drawing

Use various media to draw, including acrylic, oil painting, color pencil, and sketch. My versatility enables me to explore different artistic styles and techniques.

Videography

Personality

I am a highly creative and introspective individual who finds joy in expressing myself through storytelling. My personality is shaped by my curiosity about the human experience and my passion for understanding emotions. Writing is not just a hobby but a way of life for me; it allows me to give shape to my thoughts and create stories that resonate with others. Additionally, I enjoy visiting theaters and museums to gain inspiration for my creative projects.
